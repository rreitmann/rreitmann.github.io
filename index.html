Father, Husband, Scrum Master, Software Engineer and DevOp

Biography

I am currently looking for a new challenge as a Software Engineer. In the past I have worked in different projects as a backend developer or full stack developer as well as DevOp or Scrum Master. At DZHW, for example, I worked in an interdisciplinary team to develop a search and documentation portal for the datasets available at the Research Data Center of the DZHW. This portal continues to be developed openly at GitHub.

Before my time at the DZHW, I first worked as a consulting software engineer at Capgemini after studying economic mathematics at the Clausthal University of Technology. After my time at Capgemini, I was employed for a short time as IT coordinator at KKH. I then moved to Zühlke Engineering, where I again worked as a consulting software engineer.

During the time at Zühlke I became mentally ill at the end of 2011. In the following recovery period my son Tammo was born in 2013. In 2016 my second son Michel was born and completed our family.

Publications

Pareto Optimal Paths in Digital Street Maps

This diploma thesis tries to find an answer to the question how good the heuristical determined routes of an onboard device are. It compares pareto-optimal routes calculated with the A* algorithm with the routes calculated by an onboard device.
