Biography

I am currently employed as DevOp and Scrum Master at the Research Data Center (FDZ) of the German Center for Higher Education and Science Research (DZHW). Since 2015, I (as part of an interdisciplinary team) have been developing a search and documentation portal for the data sets available at the FDZ. This portal is developed openly on GitHub.

Before my time at the DZHW, I first worked as a consulting software engineer at Capgemini after studying mathematical economics at the Clausthal University of Technology. After my time at Capgemini, I was employed for a short time as IT coordinator at KKH. I then moved to Zühlke Engineering, where I again worked as a consulting software engineer.

During the time at Zühlke I became seriously mentally ill at the end of 2011. In the following recovery period my son Tammo was born in 2013 and I came into contact with the DZHW, where, as mentioned at the beginning, I have been working since 2015. My second son Michel was born in 2016.
